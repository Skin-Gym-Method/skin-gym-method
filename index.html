Questions

Things people ask us.

Do I need to offer specific treatments to use this system?

No. The framework works with whatever services you already offer. The modules teach you how to sequence and combine your existing treatments — not add new ones.

What if I'm a solo provider with no team?

The team module is still valuable — it shows you how to build plans that draw on your full service offering. But the consultation script, pricing, and retention modules work completely independently for solo providers.

Do I need to use RepeatMD for the follow-up system?

RepeatMD is what we use and recommend, but the follow-up system can be adapted to any platform that lets you send plan documents digitally. The strategy is platform-agnostic.

How quickly can I implement this?

Most providers are presenting their first skin plan within a week. The script and templates mean you don't have to figure anything out — you just follow the system.

What if I have questions after going through the course?

The Core + Elite option includes 30-day email Q&A access, a 30-min call with a practicing nurse injector who sells skin plans daily, and a 30-min call with a master aesthetician with decades of industry experience. The Core option is self-contained — the scripts and templates are designed to be clear enough to implement on your own.
